About Us

We are the students of IIT Mandi, have taken course named Applied database practicum. We are doing project under guidence of our respected professor Varun Dutt sir. Our project is about Conference room booking . In this project, we have created a website application where users can register and login with essential information. After login, a user can choose a conference room from the available list of conference rooms on the home page. Faculty login has high priority than that of student login for multiple Rooms and different slots in a day.
